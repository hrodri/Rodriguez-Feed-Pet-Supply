Here at Rodriguez Feed & Pet Supply we strive to have great customer satisfaction. We want our customers to leave having experienced fair prices for quality goods. We have also made it our personal mission to teach our customers the best way to take care of their pets. We like to consider our customers our own family.
-Hernán Rodriguez(CEO)

We sell:
Animal equipment like fish tanks
- Farming tools and machinery including chainsaws and mowers
- Fine quality clothing and carpets made from local materials such as wool
- Wide selection of quality dog and cat food. (Over 30 premium brands)
- Live insects and quality feed for exotic animals
- Bulk feeds for large animals
- A wide array of horse tack and polo gear
- Reptiles and other exotic animals not offered elsewhere

Rodriguez Feed & Pet Supply cares for the community of Dixon and in turn will help FFA students widen their knowledge and aid them during annual May Fair competitions. We are huge supporters of our local farmers and sell the fresh feed they grow in our barn out back. A weekly farmers market out front also sells local fruits and vegetables
